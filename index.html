Meet the Team


A.J. Jepson, CEO/CTO

A.J. has had a love of video games and art since she was a young child. She attended the International Academy of Design & Technology to obtain her degree in order to pursue her passion. SHe is an accomplished artist and programmer, creating many portraits, games, and various other works of art, over the years. "Art isn't a job. It's a way of life."


Tony Withrow, CEO/Art Director

Co-founder of TwinRayj Studios Tony is an asset designer who started making games in 2009. He's a west coast native and enjoys playing games as much as making them.


Luke Elden, Marketing Director

Luke is a UK graduate pursuing his passion in gaming via Social Media and Marketing. During his degree in Public Services, he learned how to run a local council campaign using tools such as Social Media for the marketing aspect. Also, during his years of coaching Soccer, he ran the social media accounts for the teams coached to help with recruitment, statistics and appeal. He is now excited at the prospect of using his marketing knowledge within the Gaming Industry.



Taylor Hewitt, Concept Artist I

Taylor is a recent graduate from the University of Wisconsin-Stout with a BFA in Entertainment Design, with a concentration in Animation. An aspiring illustrator and conceptual artist, her primary interest in art is digital painting with themes of epic high fantasy and character/creature design taking a forefront. Taylor uses Photoshop CC as her program of choice, but has dabbled in Paint Tool Sai and various traditional mediums like pen and ink, favoring a glass dip pen with colored inks, colored pencils, and the occasional oil pastel or paint. In her free time she can be seen making costumes for renaissance festivals or anime / sci-fi / comic conventions, drawing, working with her collection of reptiles and animals, and simply taking the time to enjoy a good round of music.


Moses Maiello, Concept Artist I

Moses wants nothing more than to shape the world into one of utter fantasy. He holds a degree in animation and illustration, hoping to use that to bring dragons, angels, werewolves and the like closer to reality. Always chasing excitement and intensity in his art, it's hard to believe he doesn't drink coffee.


Dakota Marquardt, Concept Artist I

Dakota Marquardt is a 21 year old digital artist who studied Animation & Illustration at SUNY Fredonia for her bachelor's degree. Originally, she had wanted to specifically go into animation after watching the making of Tim Burton's 9, but soon found that conceptual development in video games held stronger interest to her, which led her to the field of game art. She is an avid gamer who enjoys RPGs, and draws inspiration for her work from many sources of the horror genre. She is also the proud owner of a snaggletooth cat named Daisy and loves birds.


Lauren Coop, Social Media Marketer

Lauren is currently a student obtaining her degree in PR and Marketing in Manchester, UK having finished her placement year for Bosch. Social Media is the main passion Lauren is pursuing and therefore having worked for a well established popular company, then the next challenge is to advertise and represent a company whose growth is fast approaching.

Thomas Cutrara II, Graphic Designer

Thomas Cutrara II is a growing artist and currently a marketing coordinator at TwinRayj Studios. He is a student at Columbia College in Chicago going towards a Bachelor's in Game Art. Thomas is a twin so he's constantly surrounded by people in his everyday life. He loves to play video games, draw, and hang with friends in his free time.
